My Portfolio

DELIVERISTA WEB AND MOBILE APPLICATION

This is my capstone project during my college. Deliverista was designed to centralize the online ordering services on its platform that has a certain feature that is accurate to the online ordering and delivery services between retailers and wholesalers. The developed project was created to maintain a good relationship with the customer by providing them with quality products and services that will offer a wide variety of quality items for the customers. Many people find it difficult to shop grocery products, especially those who have tasks at home. The developed project will help customers to shop for their grocery products by browsing their computers or smartphones.

FOOD EXPRESS SYSTEM

Food Express System is an web application for the customer to order their foods and drinks through this machine. Using Google App Script and Google Docs. The purpose of this project is to lessen the waiting queue to order and buy their meals on a restaurants or fastfood chain. It displays the Food and Drinks that they wanted to order

KAPECO APPLICATION

KAPECO is an Online Application where the user can buy coffee in the store to reduce the number of people in the line to the cashier, the application will help the user needs to order and then show it to the cashier. KAPECO is an online platform or mobile platform that can be accessed by the use of mobile devices and internet connectivity.
